Research Director
Partners Behavioral Health Management - Gastonia, NC

Flexible Work Location: Gastonia, Elkin, or Hickory, NC

The research director will use quasi-experimental and experimental study designs to enable fact-based decision making. These analytics inform identification of opportunities, risk assessment, healthcare benefits utilization, differences between select providers and methods to improve services. Much of the data that requires analysis is already housed in a central database.
The Director will support designated team members already responsible for meeting the analytic and reporting needs for the State of N. Carolina, our Executive Leadership Team, and others to promote success to our client's served. The Director needs to understand, prioritize, and plan the stakeholders' information needs and lead the delivery of analyses to these stakeholders.

Primary responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:

  • Develops study designs and analytical plans, as applicable.
  • Performs, coordinates, and facilitates clinical effectiveness initiatives (incorporating quality improvement & patient safety) to achieve organizational goals. Works collaboratively with the clinical leadership & other leadership teams. Leverage existing processes for tracking and prioritizing deliverables, suggesting enhancements as needed
  • Ensure each report/analysis deliverable aligns with upfront assumptions and overall business context (and bring together relevant SMEs [Analytics, Actuary, Reporting, etc] as needed to synthesize results)
  • Leverage performance data as the basis for pay-for-performance, tiered networks, narrow networks and/or public reporting systems
  • Develop predictive models to identify high risk populations
  • Maintain expertise and visibility into trends within healthcare data
  • Provides content knowledge in the interpretation, implementation, and maintenance of standards to match external requirements (e.g., URAC, State of N. Carolina, CMS, Etc.)
  • Works collaboratively within an interdisciplinary team of Quality, Clinical, Information Technology, and Provider colleagues to ensure shared understanding and communication of important information.
  • Collaborating with the clinicians, and others to support the research functions that bring high quality reports, deliverables, and submissions to peer-reviewed publications or presentations to the state or at professional meetings.
  • Prepare local and national presentations at the direction of the Chief Clinical Officer, transforming raw clinical data and outcomes into concise material.
  • Research, identify and address barriers and facilitators for effective dissemination and implementation and determine the relationship between the intervention and its impact.
  • Prepares and writes technical reports, papers, summaries and briefings.
  • Demonstrates exceptional communication skills, and proven ability to interact effectively with constituents of the organization, including clinical staff, information technology staff, claims staff, and Executive Leadership Team.
  • Designs and develops dashboards and other innovative data visualization using business intelligence tools.

Required Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

  • Results-oriented with a high attention to detail, ability to multi-task, and meet deadlines in a demanding, fast-paced environment.
  • Project management, presentation and reporting responsibility would be advantageous to this role.
  • Board experience within a managed care environment.
  • Reputation as an influential communicator and creative problem solver, with successful examples of partnering across functions within an organization.
  • References who will speak to leadership competencies, collaboration, emotional intelligence and other Partners "cultural fit" requirements for our executive leaders.
  • Superior critical thinking, analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Skill in driving results, thinking strategically and executing strategy effectively; thinking at an enterprise level.
  • Ability to contribute to a strong team-based environment. Ability to work with a large geographic area and work remotely.
