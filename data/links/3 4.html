Chief Credit & Risk Officer - Americas
Cisco Systems, Inc. - Raleigh, NC
Additional Location(s) or Information: Research Triangle Park, Other US cities with international airport considered for the right candidate

Job Category: Finance

Level of Experience: Experienced - Manager

Requisition #: R991493

Description:

The Business Entity
Cisco’s Global Credit & Risk Management team reports into Cisco Capital. Cisco Capital is a global leader in providing financial & outcome based solutions for Cisco’s global customers & partners.

The Team
Cisco’s Global Credit & Risk Management (C&RM) team oversees & manages the extension of credit on behalf of Cisco for over $45B in revenues via our Trade & Channel programs & $10B in term leases & loans via Cisco Capital that covers over 100 countries. We are looking for a dynamic business leader with a functional expertise in Credit & Risk Management to lead our Americas region. This key leadership role must be able to balance informed risk taking & enablement to support of Cisco’s goals. We value strong analytical, communication, collaboration & talent development skills as we continue to accelerate our growing C&RM teams capabilities.

Role & Responsibilities
• Responsible for managing a team of +25 experienced Term, Channel & Trade Credit & Risk Managers throughout North & South America that oversees a multi-billion dollar portfolio
• Overseeing underwriting standards within Cisco’s Credit & Risk Policy & the risk tolerances set by the corporation including the active monitoring, reviewing, managing and reporting on counterparty risk within the region
• Serve as a senior decision maker, adviser and business partner to Cisco Executives surrounding managing and mitigating risk.
• Proactive engagement with C&RM’s Portfolio Management function to actively manage Cisco large exposures & areas of risk concentration through our analytical models
• Actively engage with key business partners including Cisco’s Americas CFO, Cisco Capital’s Americas head of Sales, Operations, Finance, Legal & other key functional areas.
Minimum Qualifications
• +10 of financial services experience with minimum of 8 years direct comprehensive underwriting, lending, structuring and workout experience
• +5 years of large team management experience
• Possess advanced understanding of Americas’ economies, sovereign dynamics, FX and in-region geopolitical issues
• Proven communication & collaborations skills & the ability to work across boundaries & functions at executives levels
• Demonstrated analytical, decision-making and effective problem solving skills
• Advanced Analytical Skills: Proficient in Moodys’ Analytics tools, MS Excel, statistics & modeling
Desired Skills
• MBA Preferred
• Advanced PowerPoint skills
